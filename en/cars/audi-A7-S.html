AUDI A7 S-LINE - Weeding Edition

Carvia - AUDI A7 S-LINE - Weeding Edition

The new Audi A7 will appeal to those looking for limousine-style luxury with an injection of distinctive flair, and Audi has taken it to a higher level than ever before. The Audi A7 has a sumptuous interior that looks and feels as if it was lifted straight from a larger Audi A8, including the latest Audi multimedia system with two screens. Moreover, like the Audi A8, the engines of the Audi A7 Sportback use 48-volt soft-hybrid technology that allows for emission-free coasting, energy recovery when braking, more responsive performance and a smarter stop-start system.

The Audi A7 infotainment system is good news and bad news. It consists of two touch screens: top 10.1 inch and radio mirroring, satellite navigation and smartphone; The 8.6-inch screen below is reserved for air conditioning, heated seats, and a few other occasional functions. We like the Audi Virtual Cockpit digital dials. They are standard on every A7 and again the screen is super sharp. The system allows you to very easily customize the information you want to see using the handy buttons on the steering wheel. The new Audi A7 S-line model is available in our rental shop.
