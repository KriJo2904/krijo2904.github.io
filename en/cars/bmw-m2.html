BMW G11 730d xDrive M-Pakiet

Carvia - BMW G11 730d xDrive M-Pakiet

BMW M2 Competition, or in other words - M2 on steroids. The model, initially presented in 2015, immediately became a sales hit, and in 2018 it received the Competition version - even more powerful and even more uncompromising. The car has a new engine, straight from the BMW M3 and M4, a changed suspension and added sports accents such as aggressive air intakes or mirrors characteristic of M models. Now the car is on the offer of our rental company.

The BMW M2 is one of those cars where excuses are made to drive it. It doesn't matter if it's a trip out of town or a simple drive to the store - you just want to drive this car. Under the hood you will find a 3-liter engine with 410 horsepower. Thanks to the double-clutch gearbox, the car we rented can accelerate to 100 km / h in 4.4 seconds. But the M2 is about more than just straight-line speed. Depending on the mood, the car can be taken quickly into corners or can be thrown around happily.

The center of the car is a classic BMW - nothing to distract us from the road and driving. In the car, it is very easy to take a comfortable position behind the wheel, thanks to the well-profiled seats, which also provide lateral support when taking faster turns. Additionally, the use of high-quality materials and the harman / kardon sound system means that when renting this car you don't need much time to get used to its interior.
