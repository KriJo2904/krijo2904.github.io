About us

A company created out of passion

Being passionate about comfortable and fast driving, we decided to create a unique premium car rental company. Our offer includes not only luxury limousines, but also cars that provide a lot of fun and pleasure. A wide range of well-known brands gives you a lot of room to choose a car that meets your expectations.


We understand that you are in a hurry and do not have time for trifles. We are also not meticulous. We do not charge for a slight delay and washing the car. We serve our clients in the way we would like to be ourselves.


We have an exceptionally carefully selected range of sports and luxury cars. This means that car rental in Carvia will allow you to make your dreams of traveling in an elegant Mercedes, a super fast Porsche or the legendary Range Rover come true. We do not accept compromises, therefore we offer our clients only premium vehicles, which are a symbol of extravagance, and excellent taste at the same time. Taking advantage of the offer of our car rental company is also a good idea when you plan to buy your own high-end car. Before you decide on such an investment, renting sports and luxury cars will allow you to test specific models on the road and compare them with each other. You can make a reservation conveniently online, and if you have any questions about premium cars or want to advise on the choice, please contact us by phone.
